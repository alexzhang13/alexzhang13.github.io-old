Alex L. Zhang

4th Year Undergrad at Princeton University

Hey! I’m a fourth year undergraduate student at Princeton University studying computer science and math. Right now, I’m particularly interested in understanding grounding and guiding agent behavior in different data modalities to create more reliable, interpretable, and less data hungry AI models. I’m also very interested in efficient training and inference of AI models at a systems-level, particularly for generative models.

  • I am fortunate enough to be advised by Professor Karthik Narasimhan, Dr. Khanh Nguyen, and Professor Kai Li.
  • I also co-founded and am the president of AI@Princeton, the undergraduate AI student organization at Princeton. I also helped organize the first AI Tiger Trek.

Finally, I just finished reading through all 3584 abstracts from NeurIPS 2023 and have compiled my notes here. I also keep a list of papers on World Models. Feel free to reach out to talk through my email at [x]@princeton.edu where [x]=alzhang.
